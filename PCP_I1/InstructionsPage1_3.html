{{ block title }} Instructions - Part 1 {{ endblock }} {{ block content }}

  • There will be 10 rounds, and in all rounds you will begin with a new endowment of 20 tokens, each of which can either be kept or contributed to the project. The 3 other players in your group will also have 20 tokens.
  • Everybody earns money in the same manner:
    • 1 token for each token kept
    • 0.4 tokens for each token contributed
    • 0.4 tokens for each token contributed by the 3 other players
  • At the start of a new round, you will be given a new endowment of 20 tokens. You are free to change the numbers of tokens kept and contributed from round to round.
  • Note: You will be matched with the same players in all rounds.
