Example

Suppose you have 20 tokens for the round, and the earnings from tokens kept, contributed, and contributed by the others are 10, 5, and 5 respectively.

  • If you keep all 20 tokens, then your earnings will be:
    • 20 from the tokens kept, plus 0.4 times the number of tokens contributed by the other players in your group.
  • If you contribute all tokens, then your earnings will be:
    • 0.4 * 20 = 8 from the tokens contributed, plus 0.4 times the number of tokens contribute by the other players in your group.
  • If you keep 10 and contribute 10, then your earnings will be:
    • 10 * 1 = 10 from the token kept, plus 10 x 0.4 = 4 for the token contributed, plus 0.4 times the number of tokens contributed by the other players in your group.

Note: In each of the 3 above cases, what you earn from the others' contributions is:

  • 0 if the others contribute 0 tokens
  • 4 if the other players contribute 10 tokens (in total) and keep the rest
  • 8 if the other players contribute 20 tokens (in total)
  • ... etc.
